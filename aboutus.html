Who Are We?

"I like my classical music like I like my rock and roll. punchy and poppy" - Arsh K.

Classical Counterpart first started as a simple project in high school, with four kids - Emma, Liam, Arsh, and Matthew - wanting to do something different for their school talent show. A modern pop/rock mix on classical pieces of music. Project became hobby, and soon enough, they started playing locally around the central PA region. In 2017, they got signed by Def Jam Recordings and soon started gaining recognition. Now they're pumping out albums every year and have more fans than ever, along with an upcoming world tour.
