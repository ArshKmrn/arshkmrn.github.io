Learn about our classes

"The South Western School District is dedicated to providing a continually improving educational environment, through a cooperative effort with the family and the entire community, in which all students are encouraged and expected to achieve their full potential, to express themselves clearly, to think reflectively, and to interact responsibly in preparation for lifelong personal growth."

Digital Media

A class that will help students understand the differences in software that will allow you to capture, edit, and share audio as well as how to use the software effectively.

Graphics 1

A class that will help students understand the technical illustration methods used to create graphics communications products.


Metal Technology

A class that will help students understand safe practice of machinery that applies to the manufacturing of consumable goods.


Intro To Engineering Design

A class that helps students understand the engineering design process and applying math, science, and engineering standards to identify and design solutions to a variety of real problems.


Principles of Engineering

This class engages and challenges students to explore a broad range of engineering topics, including mechanisms, the strength of materials and structures, automation, and motion.


Power Technology


This class helps students learn the design and creation of energy control systems that do work for us in order to make our everyday lives easier.


Practical Electronics

This class helps students learn how to use electronic lab equipment for designing, constructing, testing, and troubleshooting all types of electrical circuits.



Wood 1

This class helps students understand the various techniques that go into harvesting and understanding the nuances of various types of wood.



Applied Manufacturing

This class helps students understand the various types of machines and processes that help transform or change materials or substances into new products.


Graphics 2

Students in this class will grow their skills in various digital illustration methods used for graphical communication purposes.


Wood and Manufacturing 2

This class helps build on previously built skills by talking about manufacturing industry standards and various other techniques and processes to help make a good final product.
